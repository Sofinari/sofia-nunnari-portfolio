EduPet Aquarium Manual

2023 29/12
This project is a SUPSI didactic work from the LAB IV course.

This project initially conceived for a single aquarium manual turned into a real journey for the creation of the EduPet brand.

EduPet was born out of the need to educate prospective new pet owners in their care taking proper responsibility that many times eludes aclittle-informed pet owners about the new furry, scaly, feathered friend to bring home.

The manual is an A5 size, specially printed with recycled paper, the software used for this project is Adobe InDesign. The photos of the fish featured in the preview were taken by me in my aquarium.
