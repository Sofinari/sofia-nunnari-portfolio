My crypto postage stamps collection

2023 29/12
This project is a SUPSI didactic work from the LAB II course.

This project was inspired by the new “Swiss Crypto Stamp” mail collection.

The concept of these artefacts was to create crypto-stamps in collaboration with the post office as artist (the collaboration is not real, it is only for the idea of the project!).

Moreover, the collection was designed for a whole year as it was divided into four parts (seasonal so spring, summer, etc.), and each season there was a collaboration with an association (one different from the other) for the defence and protection of the environment/animals.
